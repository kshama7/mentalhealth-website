Our story

Mental health care, built for us

Happy Pill was founded in 2021 to meet the unique mental health needs of South Asians across the globe — with a safe space to tell your story.

Start your journey Meet our counsellors
What we believe

Therapy is for everyone

The uses of therapy extend far beyond crisis and tragedy. It helps you tap into your own wisdom and thrive.

♥

Safe & judgment-free

We create a supportive environment where you can share without fear of being judged or misunderstood.

✦

Culturally aware

Our counsellors understand the cultural nuances that shape mental health in South Asian families and communities.

●

Verified professionals

Every psychologist, counsellor, and therapist on the platform is licensed and vetted by our clinical team.

What you can talk about

Whatever's on your mind

Our team brings a diverse set of expertise across stress, anxiety, depression, trauma, and bereavement, as well as coping with relationship challenges, work issues, daily stressors, and life transitions.

We believe mental health therapy is universally beneficial — for anyone, at any stage of life.
